L3-L4-L5

No spinal flexion under load. No passive lumbar hyperextension at end range. Left leg radiating sensation = stop immediately. Ab wheel: exhale rolling out, brace before end range.

Medial elbow

Inner elbow ache during or after = reduce pulling volume 30% that week. No weighted pull-ups weeks 1–2. Week 3: +10kg only if zero elbow sensation all of weeks 1–2. Max weight (+30kg) not before month 2.

Handstand balance cue (key insight)

Front-to-back instability = wrist flexion/extension control. Falling forward: press fingertips harder. Falling back: press heel of hand harder. Never grip the whole hand equally — that removes the control mechanism.

Ab wheel progression

Knees: full extension, high volume, no restrictions. Feet: start with hands elevated on box (shin height) week 2. Lower box by one notch per week. Floor-level from feet only when zero lumbar sensation at all previous heights.

Straight-arm / tendon rule

Muscle soreness = ok. Any joint sensation (elbow, wrist, shoulder) = reduce load 30% and hold one extra week. Connective tissue adapts 3–4x slower than muscle. Never skip wrist conditioning on Tuesday.

General

3 min rest between skill sets. 2 min between strength sets. No two heavy pulling days back to back. Sleep 7–9h — connective tissue recovers during sleep, not training.
